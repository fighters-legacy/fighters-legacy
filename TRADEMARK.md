- **Build and package faithful, unmodified releases from our source** — for example a

  discussion.**

## You must rebrand (or ask first)

- **Modified versions.** If you change the code, assets, or behavior in a way that affects
  the user-facing product, distribute it under a **different name** so users aren't misled
  about what they're running. (This is the standard free-software mechanism, e.g.
  Firefox → IceWeasel, Chromium → Chrome.)
- **Anything implying endorsement or affiliation.** Don't use the name or logo in a way
  that suggests your project, build, service, or product is official or endorsed by
  MKZ Systems LLC when it isn't.
- **Confusingly similar names, domains, app-store listings, or merchandise**, or use of
  the **logo as your own brand**. Don't alter the logo or incorporate it into your own
  marks.

## Practical guidance

- A faithful rebuild: **keep the name.** A fork or a build with changed gameplay,
  bundled different content, or altered branding: **pick your own name** (you may still
  state, factually, that it is "based on Fighters Legacy").
- When in doubt, or to request permission for a use not covered above, contact
